Billing Profiles

add_circle_outline Create new billing profile

A default billing profile is applied to all new user accounts that have identified “monthly” or “annual” subscriptions. “InvoiceMe” accounts will have a custom billing profile created at the time of activation by the SaaS administrator. Changes made to default profiles will automatically be applied to all customer accounts except those for which a billing profile has been modified or created by the SaaS administrator.
